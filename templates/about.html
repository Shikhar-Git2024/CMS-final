About Us

Sugam Darshan is an AI-powered Temple Crowd Management System developed with the goal of improving the pilgrimage experience using modern technology.

The system analyzes video feeds and visitor data to estimate crowd density, classify risk levels, and provide intelligent recommendations for movement control. It serves both temple administrators and devotees by providing accurate insights.

What We Do

  • Predict crowd levels using AI models
  • Display real-time risk alerts
  • Assist authorities in crowd flow management
  • Provide pilgrims with safe darshan guidance

Our project combines Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Web Technologies to create a scalable solution suitable for temples across India.
